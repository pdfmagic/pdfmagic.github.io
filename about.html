Our Story and Mission

PDFConvertMagic started as a simple idea: to provide free and easy PDF tools for everyone. We noticed that many websites offer paid services or require registration, so we decided to be the free and simple alternative.

We believe that digital tools should be free for everyone. That's why we built a platform that works directly in your browser, without installing software, and with the same quality as paid tools.

Our tools work locally on your device. Your files remain private and are never sent to us. We put your privacy first.
